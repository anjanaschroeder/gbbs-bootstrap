Meet the Judges

Mary Berry is a leading British cookbook writer and Paul Hollywood is a top artisan baker.Together with their cheeky hosts, Paul and Mary are on the search for Britain's star amateur baker and making sure they encourage along the way!

Meet the Hosts

Writer, broadcaster, and performer are among the hats that hosts Sue Perkins and Mel Giedroyc wear. The pair work seamlessly to bring comic relief to the gingham alters. They offer a hand or two to ensure contestants' bakes are finished in time . And they do this all while teasing Paul and Mary tirelessly.
