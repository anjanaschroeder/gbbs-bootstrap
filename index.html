On Your Marks, Get Set, Baaake!

The Great British Baking Show

Signature

The first challenge is one that the contestants have had time to practice and perfect. Each baker has the same amount of time to get their bakes done and show off their skills.



Showstopper

The showstopper is the ultimate and final challenge. Each contestant should have practiced his or her recipe so much so that they're dreaming about it! Again, each baker has a fixed amount of time to complete their bakes
and wow the judges.



Technical

The technical challenge is meant to test the contestants' technical baking knowledge. Mary and Paul take turns presenting their respective recipes to the contestants. These recipes are short, sweet, simple, and often leave bakers scratching their heads and banking on the baking gods.
